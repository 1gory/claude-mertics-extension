PRIVACY POLICY FOR CLAUDE METRICS

Last updated: [Current Date]

Claude Metrics is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your information.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT
Claude Metrics only collects and stores data locally on your device:
- Time spent on claude.ai website
- Session timestamps and durations
- Usage statistics for chart display

DATA STORAGE
All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

DATA USAGE
The collected data is used solely to:
- Display your usage statistics
- Generate activity charts
- Track session history

DATA SHARING
We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. All information remains on your local device.

DATA DELETION
You can delete all stored data at any time using the "Reset All Data" button in the extension popup.
